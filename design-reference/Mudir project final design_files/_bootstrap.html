Mudir

Design Summary

Brand identity, and a page-by-page walkthrough of the Owner, Manager, Admin panels and marketing site.

Branding & logo

Mark: a single continuous monoline "vine" stroke — two connected loops rising into a diagonal ascender, topped with a floating dot (a tittle, echoing task-completion). Shown in the three brand colorways: lime background/near-black mark, white background/deep forest-green mark, dark background/lime mark. Brand name: Mudir (مدير) — Arabic for "manager/director." Type: Baloo 2 (headings/wordmark), Inter (body), JetBrains Mono (labels/tags). Palette: deep forest green #013F32 (primary), lime/chartreuse #E7FE25 (accent, used sparingly), near-black #161616 (ink), near-white #FDFDFD (background), plus functional colors — green #37B788 (pass/done), amber #E0A23B (pending), coral #E8697C (missed/fail), indigo #7C86E8 (scheduled/info).
